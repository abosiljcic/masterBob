Wallpapering

Wallpapers, which once adorned nearly every home and apartment, had been replaced by smooth, painted walls for many years. Today, they are making a strong comeback in a completely new form, becoming increasingly popular in interiors. These modern wallpapers are far more durable, often washable, easier to install, and most importantly, extremely fashionable compared to those of the past.

Why is it worth hiring a professional to wallpaper your walls?

Applying wallpaper requires more than just basic skills; it demands patience, precision, and the right tools. A thorough understanding of different types of wallpaper and adhesives is crucial, as using the wrong adhesive can compromise both the appearance and durability of the wallpaper. A skilled professional can offer valuable guidance on selecting the ideal wallpaper type and pattern for your space. Professionals also excel at wall preparation, ensuring a smooth application that enhances the wallpaper's aesthetic and longevity. Their expertise is particularly beneficial for installing complex patterns or specialty wallpapers such as textile, vinyl, or fiberglass.

Wallpapering cost

Wallpaper prices vary widely, primarily depending on the complexity of the pattern. Designer wallpapers are usually the most expensive. Unlike some other materials, wallpapers are not typically sold by the square meter, except for larger photo wallpapers that come in puzzle-like pieces. They are generally available in rolls of 10 meters long and 75 centimeters wide. Regular wallpaper prices range from 5 to 23 EUR, with some high-end options costing up to 75 EUR or more. You can either apply the wallpaper yourself or hire a professional. The cost for wallpaper installation ranges between 4 and 6 EUR per square meter.
