Painting the ceiling

The first step in any renovation is painting the ceiling. Traditionally, white is the preferred color for ceilings in houses or apartments, but this is not a hard and fast rule. Increasingly, people are opting for colorful paints to complement the walls or achieve a specific visual effect. If you choose a colorful ceiling, keep in mind that different colors will influence the perception of the room, making it appear either smaller or larger.

Is it worth painting the ceiling yourself?

There are many step-by-step guides available online that show how to paint a ceiling. However, before deciding to paint the ceiling yourself, consider whether you are confident in your ability to do it neatly and ensure a lasting finish. Any mistakes could require additional work and more materials. Unlike walls, ceiling imperfections are difficult to conceal. As a result, the cost of painting the ceiling yourself might end up being higher than hiring a professional. Remember, you are investing not just money, but also your time. Professional painters possess the necessary knowledge, experience, and skills to paint ceilings effectively. Their work will likely be more durable and of higher quality. Moreover, they will complete the task in much less time. Painting is a relatively inexpensive service, with the cost of painting a ceiling being around a few zlotys per square meter.

Painting the ceiling cost

The cost of painting largely depends on the size and complexity of the surface to be painted. Using multiple colors or creating decorative elements (e.g., accents around windows and doors) can also increase the price.
