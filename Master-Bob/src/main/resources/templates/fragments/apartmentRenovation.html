Apartment renovation

Renovating an apartment, while often a long-anticipated and carefully planned endeavor, can present several challenges. The most common issues include:

  • A budget that rarely covers all desired improvements.
  • Difficulty in finding a reliable renovation team.
  • Space limitations, such as small or irregularly shaped rooms, or complications in obtaining permits for major changes.

Despite these hurdles, starting with thorough planning can help navigate the renovation process more smoothly.

  • Professional fees

  • Material choices

    • Premium construction and finishing materials can significantly raise costs. While top-tier products often promise better quality, they can be several times more expensive. A good renovation team can offer guidance on whether the extra cost is justified.

  • Additional services

    • Major modifications, such as installing partition walls or overhauling plumbing, are complex and costly. These significant changes can considerably increase the renovation bill.
