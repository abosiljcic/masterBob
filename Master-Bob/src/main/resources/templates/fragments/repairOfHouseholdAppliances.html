Repair of household appliances

Household appliances, or household products, encompass all the equipment intended for daily use at home. This includes items such as refrigerators, dishwashers, washing machines, electric kettles, irons, dryers, and microwaves. Purchasing household appliances, especially larger ones, often represents a significant investment. Naturally, we want these devices to last for many years. However, no device is indestructible, and eventually, they are likely to break down.

Repair household appliances cost

Diagnosing a fault typically costs between 11-23 EUR, depending on whether the equipment needs to be disassembled for inspection. The cost of repairing household appliances varies based on the type of fault. Minor issues, such as replacing a washing machine's drain hose or switch, cost around 7-10 EUR. Replacing a heating element in a washing machine costs about 12 EUR, while in a dishwasher, it costs around 28 EUR. The most expensive services include replacing bearings in a washing machine or refurbishing a module in a dishwasher, which cost approximately 70 EUR.
