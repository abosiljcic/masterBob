Other plumbing services

We typically require plumbing services infrequently, usually only during failures or renovation projects. Professional plumbing support can be quite costly, so we often attempt to handle minor issues and repairs on our own. However, there are times when the task exceeds our capabilities, needing specialized knowledge, experience, or professional equipment. In these cases, it is most advisable to seek the assistance of a professional plumber.

How can a plumber help us?

We most commonly call a plumber for issues such as burst pipes, leaking seals, or dripping taps. However, the range of plumbing services extends far beyond these problems. A skilled plumber can assist with installing a bathtub, sink, toilet, and fittings (known as "white assembly"), as well as setting up a kitchen sink and tap and connecting a dishwasher. They are also equipped to handle more complex tasks, such as connecting a central heating boiler, creating a central heating system, or unclogging pipes. Additionally, plumbers are adept at locating the sources of leaks that may cause issues like flooding for neighbors. With the right equipment, they can accurately pinpoint the problem area, potentially saving us from extensive tile removal and unplanned bathroom renovations.

Professional plumbing services cost

Plumbing service price lists are quite detailed, with notable costs for white assembly, which is relevant for house building or renovation. For installing a wall-hung toilet, expect to pay around 45 EUR, a bathtub about 30 EUR, a shower cabin around 55 EUR, and a washbasin approximately 15 EUR. Higher expenses are anticipated for the installation or replacement of central heating and water and sewage systems. Traditional central heating installation is typically charged per unit, with rates around 30 EUR per point.
