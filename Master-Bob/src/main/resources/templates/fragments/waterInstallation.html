What is home plumbing?

Water installations can be divided into internal and external systems. The internal installation includes all elements hidden within walls, floors, or cabinets under sinks, such as pipes, connectors, and fittings. The external installation encompasses all final elements like taps, faucets, showerheads, and more. The starting point for a water installation is the main water pipe that brings water from the network to the house. This pipe is located below the ground's freezing zone, approximately 1.5 meters deep. Water installations are planned individually for each project, tailored to the future layout of the rooms. Although creating precise water installation designs is not formally required, having one can simplify the process. This document should detail the pipe distribution, including their diameter, type, and material, as well as the arrangement of valves, water meters, and hydrophore devices if needed. A well-thought-out plan, aligned with the future interior design, can also result in significant savings. The installation height varies for different fixtures, such as 60 cm for a sink and 120 cm for a shower. Since modifications and changes can be costly, it's advisable to plan the water installation project before construction begins. Water installations can be made from various materials. While steel and cast iron pipes were commonly used in the past, copper and plastics are now more prevalent due to their durability and resistance to rust.

Water installations cost

The costs of plumbing installations vary from house to house. They depend on the distance of individual connections, the complexity of the terrain, the layout of the rooms, and the number of vertical pipes. The price of sewage installation, including external works for an average family house, is approximately 20 EUR/m, which would mean around 1,000 EUR or more. The cost of renovating plumbing and drainage systems, including materials, for a small bathroom would be around 500 EUR. For smaller jobs, such as the fine installation of sanitary fixtures, you should allocate around 50 EUR. The costs for rough and fine plumbing installations in an average family house range from approximately 7 to 15 EUR/m.
